Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 26, 2026

At TopHabits, we value your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information Collection

We do not collect any personal identification information (such as your name, email address, or phone number) directly. The app functions primarily locally on your device.

2. Third-Party Services (RevenueCat)

We use RevenueCat to manage in-app subscriptions and purchases. RevenueCat may collect and process information regarding your purchase history to ensure your premium features are enabled across your devices. This data is handled according to RevenueCat's privacy policy.

3. Data Usage

Any data processed is used exclusively for:

  • Providing and maintaining app functionality.
  • Managing your subscriptions.
  • Preventing fraud.
  • Internal analytics to improve the app experience.

4. Data Security

We prioritize the security of your data. All communication between the app and our service providers (like RevenueCat) is encrypted using industry-standard SSL/TLS protocols.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

Since we do not store your personal data on our own servers, deletion is managed through your Google Play or Apple App Store account settings. For specific data deletion requests related to your purchase history, you can contact us.
